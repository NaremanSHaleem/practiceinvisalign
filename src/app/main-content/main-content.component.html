What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a custom-made set of clear plastic aligners or retainers that help shift your teeth into the proper position. These almost invisible braces can straighten your teeth in as little as 3 months whilst being able to easily remove them when wanting to eat, drink and attend certain occasions.

Using advanced 3-D computer imaging technology, Invisalign aligners are created and can show a complete treatment plan, from the initial positioning of your teeth to the final desired straight position.

Your aligners are changed and replaced every 2 weeks, slowly moving your teeth until the final straight result is achieved.
